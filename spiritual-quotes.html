Jeu de Mots: Post-Digital Spirit - Daily Mantras for the Inner Cyberflâneuse is a collection of playful, poetic affirmations by Marie Geneviève Cyr—designed for the in-between moments where fashion meets meditation, and presence becomes performance. These quotes act as digital mantras: accessible, shareable souvenirs of inner clarity in a hyper-connected world. Printed on caftans, they transform the body into a moving altar—each phrase a wearable invocation of transformation, humor, chaos, and calm.

Rooted in the post-digital condition, this project explores the creative process as spiritual practice: non-linear, dual, dissolving. It celebrates the blur—between past and future, screen and self, stillness and scroll. In this portal of becoming, each quote offers a soft exhale, a spark of joy, and a ritual of return.
