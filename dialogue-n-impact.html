MARIEGENEVIEVECYR
Marie Geneviève Cyr engages globally through public speaking, media consulting, and cultural dialogue to shape critical conversations at the intersection of fashion, technology, and sustainability. Her work includes keynote lectures, panel discussions, and talks at leading institutions and summits, where she shares insights on mindful design, post-internet aesthetics, and the evolving role of fashion in society. As a media consultant and guest expert, she contributes to public discourse, amplifying diverse voices and driving innovation through thought leadership and creative advocacy.
