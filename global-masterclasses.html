Marie Geneviève Cyr designs and leads dynamic masterclasses, workshops, and lectures tailored to institutions, brands, and cultural organizations worldwide. These custom programs—offered in-person or online—include 3-day to 3-week intensives, design bootcamps, thesis development sessions, and short-format lectures. Each experience merges hands-on creativity with strategic insight, fostering innovation, critical thinking, and global design perspectives. Her masterclasses have been conducted in cities including New York, Paris, London, Hangzhou, Shanghai, Beijing, Shenzhen, Guangzhou, Chengdu, Ho Chi Minh City, Ha Noi, Phnom Penh, and Santo Domingo.
