ABOUT
[ 01 ]

With over 25 years of experience spanning the fashion industry and higher education, our approach blends experimental pedagogy, radical creativity, and mindful design thinking. We support the evolution of fashion practice through research-driven insight, embodied methodologies, and cross-cultural collaboration. Whether guiding a young designer’s first collection or helping shape the future of academic programs, MGC Consulting brings clarity, purpose, and vision to every partnership.

At the heart of the consultancy is Marie Geneviève Cyr, a globally recognized Creative Director, Fashion Education Leader, and Strategic Innovation Consultant. Formerly Director of the BFA Fashion Design program at Parsons School of Design, Marie has led curriculum and leadership initiatives across Asia, Europe, and North America. Her work bridges artistic innovation, sustainability, digital transformation, and cultural diplomacy, with a deep commitment to integrating Zen philosophy, post-internet design practices, and radical pedagogy into the creative process.

Through MGC Consulting, she continues to empower a global network of students, institutions, and cultural leaders who are reimagining fashion as a space of care, identity, and transformational impact.
